    About

    Who we are

    LWF is a social enterprise committed to transforming the healthcare landscape through Continuous Professional Development (CPD) courses, top-notch Soft skills training in healthcare care, tailored age-appropriate SRHR information and services and promoting Socio-entrepreneurship skill development in Uganda and Africa. Our integrated programs address critical areas such as Communication, Attitude, Leadership, Customer care, Professionalism/Ethics, andÂ Business planning among others through equipping healthcare providers, students and Communities with essential soft skills, Entrepreneurship knowledge, and Work readiness skills for job creation.

    We do that by leveraging expert facilitators/Mentors, Organising Interactive workshops, conferences, Online courses, Blended learning, Onsite training, Coaching & Mentorship training.

    At LWF we foster a culture of excellence, Innovation & Team work ultimately transforming the healthcare landscape and improving patient outcomes in Uganda.

    WHAT DRIVES US

    At LWF, we are driven by a profound passion to transform the healthcare landscape by empowering healthcare providers with essential soft skills, Entrepreneurship mindset and work readiness competencies.

    We believe that by investing in the growth and development of these dedicated professionals, we can collectively enhance patient outcomes, foster a culture of excellence and contribute to a healthier, more resilient community.

    LWF Vision

    To become a One stop Center for Continuous Professional Development services in Soft skills, Entrepreneurship & Work Readiness Programs that elevate quality of Patients care globally.

    LWF Mission Statement

    To unlock full potential of healthcare Professionals through a Collaborative and Innovative Approach, delivering high-impact Soft skills, Entrepreneurship & Work Readiness Programs that foster excellence in Patient care, drive innovation, and promote personal growth.

    AREAS OF FOCUS

    • To Improve patient care through Top-notch soft skills training in health care.
    • To foster Social Entrepreneurship Skills Development among healthcare providers to innovate sustainable solutions in health for job creation.
    • To provide Continuous Professional Development (CPD) courses and Career mentorship to Practicing and graduating healthcare providers.
    • To enhance Work readiness skills for Graduate Nurses & Midwives,Doctors & Allied Health Professionals.
    • To Enhance professional caregiversâ training for Nurses & Aspiring caregiver providers for the elderly, sickly, and vulnerable Children.
    • To provide Customized and Comprehensive SRHR information and services including HIV prevention and care programs for adolescents & youths in learning institutions.

    Services

    What we do offer

    Improved Patient Care through Top-notch soft skills training in Health Care

    Communication Skills in health care, Leadership, Critical thinking and problem-solving, Attitude and Mindset Change, Customer Care, Financial Literacy, Self-Love and Care, Personal Awareness, and self-discovery.

    Duration

    8 weeks of Virtual & Physical

    Social Entrepreneurship Skills Development among health Care providers

    Idea generation, Design thinking, Innovation and creativity, Business management, Financial Management, Record keeping, Resource Mobilization, Business branding and social marketing, Market research, Business Planning and Business Advisory services.

    Duration

    24 weeks full package. Virtual & Physical

    Enhanced Work readiness skills for graduate Nurses, Midwives & doctors in Uganda

    Time Management, CV and cover letter and report writing, Job search support and Linkages, Interview Tips, digital literacy training, building self-esteem, work Ethics, team building, stress management, presentation skills, Adaptability, diversity and inclusion training, multi-tasking, work-life balance, Change Management, Career mentorship and coaching, conflict resolution and Management skills.

    Duration

    4 weeks of Virtual and Physical
